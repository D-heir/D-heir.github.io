Black Box Project

In our new media and tech class there is a black box. We don’t know what is inside of it or how it works. The only information we got was from our teacher Mr.Sinusas. We went through a variety of tests and experiments to think of an idea as to what was inside the box through multiple types of modeling and testing prototypes. To make this process easier we were assigned into groups of 3-4 people each group was assigned a colour such as red, green, blue, magenta, yellow, cyan. I was assigned to the green team which consisted of me (D’aire M. Sheats), James N. Carrasco, and Jaxon T. Taylor. As for our model it was a product of a simple idea.

Claim-Evidence-Reasoning:
What's Inside the Mystery Black Box?

CLAIM

A siphon mechanism could be a key feature inside of the black box.

EVIDENCE

When making our model we thought of a mechanism that produces inconsistent results like the black box. We then stumbled upon the siphon which works but only when the water is at a certain height in its container. When testing the prototype we made it proved to be inconsistent with its results thus leading us to believe that this simple mechanism may be what is inside the black box.
